Epic Games’ giveaway for 21th of October Epic Games’ new weekly giveaway is now available on the Epic Store. By Lajos Kis | Oct 21, 2021, 17:00 GMT

Among the Sleep takes you back to being a toddler again. The walking simulator, developed by Krillbite Studio, focuses on atmosphere and simple puzzle solving, and while its story is not the deepest, it's an interesting adventure to entertain you for a few hours. In the game you are in search of your missing mother and to find her, you must traverse various worlds to relive essential memories of her. But you have to do this safely, since the worlds you visit aren't the friendliest. The twisted and nigthmarish landscapes and its inhabitants are out there to get you, and since you are a toddler, your only options are to run and to hide from them. Don't think jumpscares though, Among the Sleep is more of a scary by atmosphere type, which is enhanced by the nature of walking simulators, i.e. your available actions are limited to crawling, walking, running and grabbing, all of which are at the speed of a small child.

Usually the game is sold for around €17, but now you can get it for free on the Epic Store until 17:00 GMT on the 28th of October.
