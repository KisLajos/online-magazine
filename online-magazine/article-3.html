Baba Is You Is Amazing Has Mechanics By Lajos Kis | Oct 21, 2021, 14:00 GMT

Baba Is You is a silly little game on the first glance. A small rabbit looking thing moving around one step at a time, pushing blocks to their designated spots, like good old Sokoban. But when you look around more carefully, you can actually see some words floating around on the playing field. Like "Baba", "Is" and "You". You're curious, and try to move the "You" block away and then... (pause)

That was my first experience with Baba Is You and it will be yours too. The main game mechanic is that you can move blocks around with your character, but that also includes the rules themself, which are also on the field. If you can reach a rule statement, like in the above example, "Baba Is You", you can actually break the rules, so to say. What happened in that example was that I moved away "You", and since "Baba" isn't anything anymore, you can't control the character. "Baba" has to be "You" in order to keep playing. (But that rule can also be broken, as it turns out.)

I don't want to spoil too much about the game, you have to try it for yourself, because that is the best way to experience it. The whole game is just oohs and aahs all way as you figure out the mindbending puzzles one after another.
